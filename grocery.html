Frequently asked questions

Do you deliver groceries in St. Thomas, USVI?

Yes. Top Dog Tour & Taxi provides grocery pickup, essentials delivery, and grocery stops added to rides in St. Thomas, USVI. Send your location and order details first so we can confirm timing and logistics.

Can you add a grocery stop to my airport pickup?

Yes. If you are arriving at STT, we can often add a grocery stop to your airport pickup so you do not need a separate shopping trip later. This works best when you book in advance and tell us the store, order size, passenger count, and luggage amount.

How much does grocery pickup cost?

Current grocery service options start at $20 for a grocery stop added to a ride, $25 for a small pickup or essentials order, and $35 for a larger pickup or bulk order. Unusually large requests, rush jobs, extra stops, or long waits may require confirmation.

Do you deliver to hotels and vacation rentals?

We can deliver groceries, drinks, and essentials to many hotels, villas, condos, and vacation rentals across St. Thomas. Send the address and delivery instructions before ordering so we can confirm access and availability.

Can you pick up items from stores without online ordering?

Often, yes. Message us with the store, item list, timing, and destination. We will confirm whether the request can be completed and how it should be priced.

What if an item is unavailable?

Please include acceptable substitutions when you send the request. If something important is unavailable, we will contact you through your preferred contact method when possible.
